Working Style

The pattern behind the portfolio

01

Frame the decision

Start with the business question, stakeholder, constraint, and decision threshold.

02

Build the model

Translate assumptions into calculations, scenarios, risk flags, and audit tables.

03

Show the evidence

Use dashboards, KPI cards, data tables, and validation tests to make the logic reviewable.

04

Write the recommendation

End with a memo-style answer: what to do, why, what could go wrong, and what to monitor.
